### X power X equation
To solve $x^x = c$ for $c \in R^+$
@annend

#### Solution
$$
x^x = c
$$
$$
x = c^\frac{1}{x}
$$
$$
1 = \frac{c^\frac{1}{x}}{x}
$$
$$
1 = \frac{1}{x} e^{\frac{ln(c)}{x}}
$$
$$
ln(c) = \frac{ln(c)}{x} e^{\frac{ln(c)}{x}}
$$
$$
W(ln(c)) = \frac{ln(c)}{x}
$$
$$
x = \frac{ln(c)}{W(ln(c))}
$$
